Picotutor

Spreading knowledge, ten minutes at a time.

Learn

Want to learn something? No long-term commitment is required. Picotutor is optimized for spreading knowledge in bite-sized chunks. Browse our catalog to pick the topic or tutor you like and schedule a one-on-one session that lasts from 10 to 30 minutes. At the end of it, you will emerge a little more knowledgeable to learn more. Plus, here you grade your teacher, not vise versa. Learn every day!

Teach

There is a teacher in each one of us. Know something useful? Want to spread the knowledge? Prove yourself by teaching a specific topic in a short, easy-to-digest session over the Internet! Earn feedback and appraisals from your students and create a public profile to show your cred!

About us

At Picotutor, we believe that world needs more knowledgeable people, not less. We also understand that keeping a long-term commitment to study is a challenge. That's why we want to give everyone an opportunity to learn a little each and every day.

Become part of the future of microeducation! Support us in our quest to the public release.
